Linus Torvalds

The Creator of Linux

Biography

Linus Torvalds is a Finish-American software engineer who is the creator and principal developer of Linux kernel which is the kernel of for a number of operating systemshe also created the version control system git torvalds was born on December 28 1969 in Helsinki Finland he studied computer science at the University of Helsinki and he developed the Linux kernel as a student project in 1991.Since then, the Linux kernel has become one of the most widely used kernels in the world, powering everything from a smartphone to supercomputers.
